Readmission Fee Waiver

Students who have taken time away from university can apply for readmission through their college. They should contact their respective college to be clear on readmission deadlines. EOP provides a Readmission Fee Waiver that waives the $70 application fee for students.

The Process
The student will meet with an Academic Counselor who can access the Readmission Fee Waiver in the EOP Forms Google Folder . The counselor completes the form with the student's information and gives it to the student to submit with their readmission packet.
