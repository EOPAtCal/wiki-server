EOP Email Advising

Students submit questions to the EOP office regarding academic, financial aid, sponsorship, and other matters related to their status as UCB undergrads via the advising@berkeley.edu email link that is found on the EOP homepage . This office periodically gets emails from other entities inquiring about programs and services offered by EOP. It is important for the program coordinator to respond to these inquiries in an expeditious and accurate manner.

The Process
Students and other parties submit inquiries to EOP via the advising@berkeley.edu email link. The project coordinator checks the advising@berkeley.edu email every morning and periodically through the day to review email messages and respond accordingly. Questions are answered via email and, if appropriate, students are referred to specific campus personnel, departments and/or web sites. If students request EOP verifications for the Berkeley Student Cooperative, RSF Scholarship, Berkeley Art Studio Scholarship, grad school prep fee reductions, etc., program coordinator accesses appropriate Google Doc and submits students name, SID, etc. so they may receive the particular accommodation/fee waiver/reduction. Follow-up email to students informs them that the EOP verification has been submitted (and provide them with appropriate instructions if necessary). All exchanges with UCB students, current and former, are to be documented in QUINCY.
