Berkeley Hope Scholars (BHS) EOP Counselor

Description
The designated EOP counselor serves a caseload of about 60 Cal Independent Scholars Network (CISN) students every academic year. Students are encouraged to meet semesterly with the EOP counselor for assistance in academic planning. Every semester, the BHS Director and EOP counselor perform a grade check for all students. Students who do not pass a class or are placed on academic probation are required to meet with the EOP counselor to overview their course schedule. The students housing account for books will not be set up until they complete a Semester Study List with their EOP counselor.

The counselor also helps to co-facilitate a Fall transitional course for incoming BHS students titled Ensuring Success for Foster Youth in Higher Education. This one unit p/np Social Welfare 97/197 course includes campus resources, community building and a general introduction to the research university. In addition, all incoming BHS students receive a iPAD which is used to create an interactive narrative presentation during the course.
