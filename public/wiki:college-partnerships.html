College Quick Facts for New Students

College of Natural Resources (CNR)

  • Advising Office: 260 Mulford Hall
  • Website: cnr.berkeley.edu
  • Unit minimum per semester (w/case-by-case exceptions): 13 units
  • All major requirements must be taken for a letter grade
  • Semester Limit, No Unit Limit
  • Started Cal as a Freshmen = 8 semesters
  • Started Cal as a Transfer = 4 semesters
  • AP and IB high school exams can complete requirements
  • No Foreign Language or Quantitative Reasoning requirement, except Environmental Economics and Policy majors

College of Environmental Design (CED)

  • Advising Office: 250 Wurster Hall
  • Website: http://ced.berkeley.edu/ced/students/undergraduate-advising/
  • Unit minimum per semester (w/case-by-case exceptions): 12 units
  • Semester Limit, No Unit Limit
  • Started Cal as a Freshmen = 8 semesters
  • Started Cal as a Transfer = 4 semesters
  • Exception may be requested with revised graduation plan
  • Graduation plan for each major can be found in Major Handbook online:
  • ced.berkeley.edu/advising/forms
  • Curriculum is fairly fixed, especially junior and senior year
  • Best to take Environmental Design 1 (ED 1) first semester.
  • New major in Sustainable Environmental Design is impacted. No guarantee of changing in after admission to UCB. Talk with SED major advisor.
  • AP, IB, GCE and A-Level high school exams can complete requirements
  • Most required courses must be taken for letter grade. See advisor if considering P/NP
  • Only one breadth course may be taken P/NP
  • No College-level language or quantitative breadth requirement. However, Arch, SED, and US majors do require calculus and/or stat (check major handbooks)

College of Chemistry (COC)

  • Advising Office: 121 Gilman Hall
  • Website: chemistry.berkeley.edu
  • Unit minimum per semester (w/case-by-case exceptions): 13 units
  • Each semester: 12 units must satisfy major requirements - math, science, breadth electives (including R&C + AC)
  • All technical courses must be taken for letter grade (chemistry, math, physics courses)
  • AP, IB, and A-Level exams can complete requirements

College of Engineering (COE)

  • Advising Office: 230 Bechtel Hall
  • Center for Access to Engineering Excellence: 227 Bechtel Hall
  • Tutoring for most lower division courses
  • Website: coe.berkeley.edu/ess
  • Students make appointments on this site
  • Unit minimum per semester (w/case-by-case exceptions): 12 units
  • Each semester: at least two technical courses required of the major
  • All technical courses must be taken for letter grade
  • Technical = math, science, engineering courses
  • Semester-by-semester plan for each major: coe.berkeley.edu/guide
  • Change of college information: coe.berkeley.edu/coc
  • Engineering 92, 1 unit P/NP, no work involved other than attendance
  • Course is open to anyone and helps explore engineering fields

College of Letters and Science (L&S)

  • Advising Office: 206 Evans Hall
  • Website: ls-advise.berkeley.edu
  • Unit minimum per semester (w/case-by-case exceptions): 13 units
  • Up to 1/3 of UC Berkeley units can be taken P/NP
