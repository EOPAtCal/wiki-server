Undocumented Student Program Wiki

Welcome to the Undocumented Student Program Wiki!

UC Berkeleys Undocumented Student Program (USP) (a component of the Center for Educational Equity and Excellence ) provides guidance and support to undocumented undergraduates at Cal. USP practices a holistic, multicultural, and solution-focused approach that delivers individualized service for each student. The academic counseling, legal support, financial aid resources, and extensive campus referral network provided by USP helps students develop the unique gifts and talents they each bring to the university while empowering a sense of belonging. The programs mission is to support the advancement of undocumented students within higher education and promote pathways for engaged scholarship.
