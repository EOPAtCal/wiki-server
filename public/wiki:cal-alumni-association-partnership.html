Cal Alumni Association Partnership

The Cal Alumni Association (CAA) and EOP established a partnership in Fall 2014 to provide academic support to new The Kruttschnitt Aspire Scholarship Program (KASP) and The Achievement Award Program (TAAP) scholarship recipients. EOP will work closely with both KASP and TAAP scholars to ensure that they are completing appropriate courses that will lead to major completion and career and academic goals post graduation.

The Process
Students can apply to the TAAP and KASP scholarships online . Once students are selected and awarded a scholarship they must attend a retreat at the beginning of the academic semester in Fall. The retreat is scheduled during the last week of August. In the retreat EOP will work with students around team building and transition issues from high school to college life (for example addressing Imposter Syndrome, Survivors Guilt, Depression, Fear of Success, Empty Nest Syndrome, Anxiety, Depression, and Homesickness.

TAAP and KASP Program Requirements
1.Full-time enrollment at UC Berkeley
2.Evening scholar meetings and events throughout the academic year
3.10 hours of community service and outreach per semester
4. Satisfactory Academic Progress as defined by the Office of Financial Aid and the scholars college

Application Period and Deadline
Students must apply to the TAAP scholarship before they are accepted to UC Berkeley. To be eligible for TAAP, first year applicants and transfer students must have parents who's annual income is less than $86,000 a year. Students need to complete their high school diploma, GED, or IGECT. There are no immigration status requirements for the scholarship. The student must apply to UC Berkeley to begin school in the Fall of the following year and the student needs to SIR by May 1 of that year.

KASP has the same requirements but the only significant difference is that students must identify as African American, Chicano/Latino, and/or American Indian/Alaska Native.

Scholarship Duration
Under TAAP and KASP frosh receive $6000 per year and a laptop. The scholarship is active for 8 semesters. Transfer students receive $6000 per year and a laptop the scholarship is active for 4 semesters.

Application Period and Deadline
Applications are only available online . No post-mailed, emailed, or faxed applications will be accepted. Please refer to website for updated deadlines.
