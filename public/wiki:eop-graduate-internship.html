EOP Graduate Internship

EOP serves to hire, train, and supervise graduate student(s) in Counseling or Higher Ed program for the academic year. The graduate intern(s) meet regularly with the designated EOP counselor to outline professional development goals. Additionally, graduate interns facilitate the Beyond Berkeley: Preparation for Graduate School course.

The Process
EOP counselor distributes graduate intern announcement in February of each year to local Counseling and Higher Ed programs via email (see attached announcement). Interview candidates in March-April with a committee of Peer Academic Counselors and current graduate intern. Procure office space and funding for graduate intern prior to Summer term of the academic year they will be at CE3/EOP.
