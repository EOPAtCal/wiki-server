Berkeley Student Cooperative Liaison

All EOP students, regardless of income level, are eligible to receive priority placement and a reduced security deposit for the Berkeley Student Cooperative housing system (aka, BSC, Co-ops, Coops). This priority placement affords them the opportunity for housing placement ahead of all new applicants to the system, but behind all current Co-op members and other EOP students who have already submitted an EOP verification. All applicants to the Co-ops must submit a $50 application fee. The Process
When students contact the EOP office for verification, staff must confirm their eligibility by checking Quincy and other available databases. Once EOP status is confirmed, the students EOP verification can be sent to the Berkeley Student Cooperative using the shared Student Coop EOP Verifications for Berkeley shared GoogleDoc , where EOP staff will submit the students name, email address, phone number, EOP verification date and the initials of the staff person submitting the verification. After forwarding the EOP verification to the Co-ops, EOP staff will make the appropriate entry in Quincy.

Once the student has been processed by the Berkeley Student Cooperative and a housing placement offer made, they will place an X in the first box of the Google Doc.

Periodically, the EOP liaison to the Co-ops will need to purge the Student Coop EOP Verifications for Berkeley shared GoogleDoc . Students who have been given offers by the Co-ops (those with the X in front of their names), will need to be removed and placed on another shared Google Doc entitled, Processed EOP Verifications to Co-ops. Once the adjustments have been made, both Google Docs will need to be alphabetized by last name.
