ACADEMIC WORKSHOPS

This workshop series introduces transfer students to various campus resources, opportunities, and academic strategies. These workshops are intended to provide transfer students with an opportunity to explore topics and other information that may be of interest. Sessions include a Q & A segment.

The Academic Strategy and Enrichment Workshops begin the 3rd week of the semester. They are scheduled on most Wednesdays from 12-1pm (unless otherwise noted) in room 105 Cesar Chavez.

EXAMPLES OF WORKSHOP TOPICS INCLUDE:

Undergraduate Research Opportunities

Global Poverty and Practice Minor

Travel Abroad

Enhancing Test Performance

Graduate School Panel

Financial Aid
