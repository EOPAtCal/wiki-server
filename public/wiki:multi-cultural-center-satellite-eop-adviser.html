Multi-Cultural Center (MCC) Satellite EOP Adviser

A designated EOP counselor provides drop-in advising to students who frequent the Multicultural Community Center . The counselor is on site for drop-in once a week during a 3 hour time block. Fall 2014 began as a test semester, and this may or may not continue into the Spring of 2015 and into subsequent academic years.

The Process
EOP counselor checks in with MCC Director Elisa Huerta at Elisahuerta@berkeley.edu to coordinate hours. They also bring an iPad in order to provide efficient and effective drop-in advising to students who visit the MCC.
