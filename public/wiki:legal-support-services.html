Legal Support Services

USP partners with the Human Rights Immigration Clinic at Boalt Law School to provide consultation and legal aid to undocumented students only. Allison Davenport & Olivia Layug serve as the primary contacts. The most commonly requested legal services are regarding: DACA, DACA Renewal, U & T Visas, Advanced Parole, and general consultation.

*The Process *
Allison & Olivia monitor and manage the email account: dreamers@law.berkeley.edu The USP Counselor/Coordinator manages appointment requests on the following google survey form "Legal Support Appointment Request. This form is advertised to students on the USP website and is used to coordinate appointments, office hours, etc.

If room reservations are needed for meeting space in Chavez, room 112, 115, 116 are good spaces to use. Students' appointments are recorded on the front desk calendar legal support and they are checked into Quincy when they check in for appointment in 119 Chavez. When students meet with our legal support team to do DACA or DACA Renewal, they are eligible to receive a check for $465 to DHS.
