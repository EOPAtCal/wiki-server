Laney College EOPS Advisory Board

The California Community College Commission requires each campus EOPS program to have an advisory board. The advisory board consists of members of Laney College staff, post secondary educational institutions, corporations, community services that serves the Laney College EOPS populations, Alameda County public service programs, interested alumni, and others. At Laney College, the advisory board meets twice annually, once in mid-fall and again in mid-spring, to apprise members of the current status of the various programs and services offered by EOPS, numbers of students served, areas of concern, impending changes, budget matters, etc.

The Process
As a member of Laney Colleges EOPS program advisory board, the EOP staff member will attend the bi-annual advisory board meetings and provide insights as to how UCBs EOP program can collaborate with Laneys EOPS program and best serve its students, a small number of whom are admitted to UC Berkeley each year. Additionally, EOP will get the names and contact with UCB admits to facilitate their transition to Berkeley.
